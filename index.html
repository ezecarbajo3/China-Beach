About

China Beach - Wok On The Beach

Welcome to China Beach - Wok On The Beach, a kosher Chinese restaurant located at 3919 Alton Rd in Miami Beach. The concept blends familiar Chinese comfort dishes with kosher standards, fresh preparation, and an approachable neighborhood atmosphere.

The current brand identity already communicates a few strong things: authentic flavor, generous portions, warm service, and practical convenience for dine-in, takeout, and delivery. This redesign keeps those strengths, but presents them with more clarity and polish.

Signature references from the current site include favorites such as Mongolian Duo, Beef and Broccoli Duo, Sesame Duo, Orange Peel Duo, fried rice, lo mein noodles, soups, salads, vegetable dishes, and simple sides that make the menu feel broad without losing focus.
